## English full description
Juha only had one simple errand. Then the desert woke up, the spirits got angry, and his donkey started judging every decision.

Guide Juha through a colorful survival adventure inspired by Arabian folklore and the timeless humor of clever-fool tales. Move with simple touch controls while Juha attacks automatically, then collect experience and choose upgrades as each run becomes stranger and more dangerous.

Face playful desert creatures, dust spirits, ghouls, and other supernatural trouble. Combine weapons, relics, and upgrades to create powerful builds and survive increasingly chaotic enemy waves.

Juha’s grey donkey is never far away. It may not help, but it will definitely look disappointed.

Current prototype direction includes:
• Touch movement
• Automatic combat
• Enemy waves
• Experience collection
• Character upgrades
• A humorous Arabian-folklore setting

Planned development may add more characters, unlockable weapons, challenge-based unlocks, donkey and camel progression, additional maps, bosses, and expanded stories. Planned features are not guaranteed to appear in the first public release.

Juha’s Bad Day: Habibi, Run! draws inspiration from storytelling traditions, desert adventures, jinn, ghouls, caravans, and Juha’s legendary bad luck. The game uses an original world and original visual identity.

The game is currently in development. Final content and features may change before release.
