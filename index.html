Juha’s Bad Day:

One simple errand. Several angry spirits. One very bad day.

Run, fight, collect ridiculous upgrades, and survive a colorful adventure inspired by Arabian folklore and Juha’s legendary bad luck.

Explore the game Press kit

The premise

A tiny errand becomes a mythical disaster

Juha is an ordinary man with extraordinary bad luck. What starts as a simple task quickly attracts desert creatures, angry spirits, and far more trouble than any sensible person would accept.

Prototype scope

Current or expected prototype features

Touch movement

Move Juha directly with simple mobile controls designed for landscape play.

Automatic combat

Focus on movement and survival while attacks trigger automatically.

Enemy waves

Survive escalating groups of playful supernatural enemies.

XP collection

Collect experience during a run and choose upgrades as Juha levels up.

Character upgrades

Build stronger combinations during each run.

Folklore-inspired comedy

A colorful tone inspired by Juha stories and Arabian storytelling traditions.

These items describe the intended prototype scope and must be verified against the playable build before launch.

Juha

The least prepared hero

Juha is not a chosen warrior. He is just trying to finish one errand while everything around him gets louder, stranger, and considerably more dangerous.

The donkey

Judgement included

Juha’s grey donkey has seen enough to know this was a terrible idea. Its expression says what the player is already thinking.
