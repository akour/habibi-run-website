About One Apps

One Apps is the developer of Juha’s Bad Day: Habibi, Run!, an in-development Android action roguelite built with Godot 4.

The project combines approachable mobile controls, escalating survival gameplay, and a humorous storybook world inspired by Juha tales and Arabian folklore. Its goal is to feel playful, colorful, and respectful rather than derivative of any existing film, game, or character design.

Project status

The game is in development. Public artwork on this site is placeholder concept art unless explicitly identified as gameplay.
